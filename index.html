CAMPUS LIFE

One memorable campus moment was during a "Mini Marathon" organized by UTHM, where students came together early in the morning wearing bright yellow t-shirts. The event was all about having fun, not competing, with some running, walking, and even dancing through the course. Volunteers at checkpoints handed out water, snacks, and colorful powders to splash on each other, making it feel festive and energetic. Crossing the finish line together brought a sense of accomplishment, followed by a small party with music and food, leaving everyone sweaty, colorful, and full of laughter. It was a perfect blend of fitness and fun, creating unforgettable memories.
